Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • mozzerella
  • lasagna sticks
  • beef
  • pasta sauce
  • spices

Steps

  1. boil lasagna sticks
  2. get pan and stack ingredients in following order repeat 3x
    1. sticks
    2. sauce
    3. beef/veggies
    4. cheese
  3. bake for 30 mins, cover pan with foil
